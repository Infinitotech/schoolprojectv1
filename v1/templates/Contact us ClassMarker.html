Common questions

Can I add multiple administrators?

Yes. You can create additional logins for "Assistants" to help manage your account. Each assistant can have separate permissions as required. There is no extra cost for creating Assistants. See: How to Create Assistants

My test results are not being saved?

If you are logged into ClassMarker as the administrator when taking the test, the results will not be saved because you are in preview mode.

First log out as administrator and then either log in with the credentials of a registered user, or use a direct link to take your tests and have your results saved.

Also with direct link tests, you have the option to save or not save results when assigning or editing your test settings via the Tests. Note: Only Professional 1 or Professional 2 accounts, or accounts that have purchased a credit pack can save results from direct link tests.

How do I edit my test settings?

To edit the questions in your test, go to the Tests section and select 'Edit' under your test.

If you want to edit the settings such as Time limit, Test availability, Passmark, Feedback and more, to go the Tests section and select 'Settings' under your test next to the Group or Link you wish to edit the settings for.
