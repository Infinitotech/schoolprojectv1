Instructions

Allowed image types:

.jpg .jpeg .gif .png

Allowed document types:

.doc .docx .odt .rtf .txt .pdf .ppt
.pptx .pps .ppsx .odp .xls .xlsx .csv .ods

Allowed video types:

.mp4

Allowed audio types:

.mp3

File names

File names can only contain alphanumeric characters, spaces, underscores and dashes. Apostrophes aren't allowed for example.

Max file sizes

Images: 200KB
Documents: 2MB
Video files: 5MB
Audio files: 2MB

Image dimensions

Keep under 600px wide
No height restrictions

Images larger than 200Kb can slow down page loading times and ultimately affect test times. Optimize images before uploading them to give your users an acceptable download time for your test pages.
