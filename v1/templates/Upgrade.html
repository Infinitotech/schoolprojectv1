Which option is right for me?

Professional Plans

Professional plans provide 400 or 1,000 credits per month - great for regular testing.

Professional monthly plans have no contract period so you can cancel anytime with no further charges.


Credit Packs

Credit packs fully upgrade your account just like Professional plans and your credits can be used anytime over the year.


Combine plans

You can purchase a Professional plan and later a Credit pack if you need extra Credits for a busy month. Any unused Credit pack credits will then remain on your account separately until you need them again.


How Credits work

Each time one of your tests is taken, only 1 credit is used (Certificate Downloading and Emailing Results included).
